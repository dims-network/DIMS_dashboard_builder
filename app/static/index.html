3 · Align video & data

When a session's video and time-series have different lengths, the dashboard shows dead space. For each session you can trim the video to a window you pick, or pad the time-series with zeros at the start and/or end. The bars below preview the result against a shared playback timeline; edits are non-destructive — your original files are never modified.
